# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 16, 2026
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Overview

Scribe is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Audio Data (Microphone)

**What we collect:** When you use the dictation feature, the App captures audio from your microphone.

**How we use it:** Audio is sent to third-party speech-to-text services for transcription.

**Storage:** Audio is NOT stored on your device or our servers. It is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after transcription.

### 2. Clipboard Content

**What we collect:** When you use the "select-to-transform" feature, the App reads text from your clipboard.

**How we use it:** The selected text is sent to AI services for transformation as you request.

**Storage:** Clipboard content is NOT stored. It is read only when you initiate the feature and discarded after processing.

### 3. Active Window Information

**What we collect:** The App detects which application window is currently active to provide context-aware features.

**How we use it:** This helps the App paste transcribed text into the correct application.

**Storage:** Active window information is temporarily cached during your session and not persisted.

### 4. Installed Applications

**What we collect:** The App scans for installed applications to enable app-specific features and profiles.

**How we use it:** This information helps customize the transcription experience for different applications.

**Storage:** Application information is cached locally on your device only.

### 5. API Keys

**What you provide:** You provide your own API keys for speech-to-text and AI services.

**How we store them:** API keys are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. They are NEVER transmitted to our servers.

## Third-Party Services

Your audio and text data may be sent to the following third-party services:

- **Groq:** For speech-to-text transcription
- **Other LLM providers:** For text transformation features (if configured)

Please review the privacy policies of these services, as they have their own data handling practices.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification information
- We do NOT collect usage analytics or telemetry
- We do NOT store your audio recordings
- We do NOT have access to your API keys
- We do NOT track your transcription history

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- API keys are stored using Tauri's secure storage plugin
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- All processing happens locally or through encrypted connections to third-party services

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Review what data the App stores locally
- **Delete:** Uninstall the App to remove all local data
- **Control:** Choose whether to use features that access your microphone or clipboard

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes that affect how we handle your data will require you to re-accept the updated policy.

## Offline Use

The consent and privacy features work entirely offline. Legal documents are bundled with the App and do not require an internet connection to view.

## Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the project's GitHub repository.
