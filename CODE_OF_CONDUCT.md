# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want the Trueears community to be open, respectful, and useful to contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent, but be clear and direct about technical concerns.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people.
- Share context when reporting bugs, proposing changes, or disagreeing with an approach.
- Respect privacy and never post secrets, credentials, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Trolling, baiting, or bad-faith review behavior.
- Knowingly submitting malware, credential dumps, or abusive content.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to `shayankhanx1x@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this code of conduct.
